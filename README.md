📊 Customer Insights & Revenue Analysis
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior to extract meaningful business insights that can drive data-driven decision-making. The workflow includes data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL-based analysis using PostgreSQL, and visualization through an interactive dashboard.

The goal is to understand customer spending patterns, product performance, and the impact of business strategies like discounts, subscriptions, and shipping types.

🛠️ Tech Stack

Python (EDA & Feature Engineering)

PostgreSQL (Data Storage & Analysis)

Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

📂 Key Business Questions Solved
1. Revenue Distribution by Gender

Compared total revenue generated by male vs. female customers

Helps in identifying target customer segments for marketing

2. Discount Effectiveness

Identified customers who used discounts but still spent above average

Helps evaluate whether discounts drive higher spending or reduce profit margins

3. Product Performance (Ratings)

Found top 5 products with highest average ratings

Useful for:

Promoting high-rated products

Improving low-rated ones

4. Shipping Impact on Spending

Compared average purchase between Standard vs Express shipping

Helps optimize logistics strategy and pricing models

5. Subscription Analysis

Compared spending behavior of subscribed vs non-subscribed customers

Helps answer:

Do subscriptions increase revenue?

Should business invest more in subscription models?

6. Discount Usage by Product

Identified products with highest discount usage rate

Helps:

Detect over-reliance on discounts

Optimize pricing strategies

7. Customer Segmentation

Segmented customers into:

New

Returning

Loyal

Helps in:

Personalized marketing

Retention strategies

8. Top Products by Category

Identified top 3 most purchased products in each category

Useful for:

Inventory planning

Product placement strategies

9. Repeat Buyers vs Subscription

Analyzed whether repeat customers are more likely to subscribe

Helps improve customer retention and loyalty programs

10. Revenue by Age Group

Measured revenue contribution across age groups

Helps:

Identify high-value demographics

Tailor marketing campaigns

💡 Key Business Insights

🔥 A small segment of customers contributes a large portion of revenue → focus on high-value customer retention

💸 Discounts can increase spending but may reduce margins → implement targeted discount strategies

⭐ Highly rated products indicate strong customer satisfaction → leverage for marketing and recommendations

🚚 Shipping preferences influence purchase behavior → optimize delivery options and pricing

🔁 Loyal and repeat customers are key revenue drivers → invest in loyalty programs

📦 Certain products rely heavily on discounts → re-evaluate pricing strategy

👥 Different age groups show varied spending patterns → enable demographic-based targeting

📊 Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard was created to:

Visualize key KPIs

Track customer and product trends

Provide actionable insights through filters and dynamic visuals
